every year , denver teacher schwartz passes out post-it notes to her third grade students and asks them to complete the sentence , " i wish my teacher knew ... " many of the students she teaches at doull elementary just wish they had something in common with her , that she knew soccer or video games.
" the assignment sprang from the kinds of conversations teachers have all the time , schwartz said : " what do our students need ? how can we best serve them ? " schwartz always gives her students the option to write their names on their notes or remain anonymous ; of sharing them only with her , or with the entire class
