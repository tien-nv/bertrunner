amazon has won approval from united states federal regulators to test the latest version of its delivery drone outdoors , less than a month after the e-commerce powerhouse blasted regulators for being slow to approve commercial drone testing.
the faa had earlier given the green light to an amazon prototype drone in march , but the company told united states lawmakers less than a week later that the prototype had already become obsolete while it waited more than six months for the agency 's permission.
the faa granted amazon 's request to test its new delivery drones in a letter dated wednesday , posted on the agency 's website.
seattle - based amazon has been pursuing its goal of sending packages to customers by air , using small , self - piloted aircraft , even as it faces public concern about safety and privacy
