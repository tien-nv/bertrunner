jenny wallenda , 87 , the matriarch of the famous family of high - flying circus performers , died late saturday at her home in sarasota , florida , according to family members.
jenny wallenda 's nephew , rick wallenda , said his aunt died following a lengthy illness.
jenny wallenda was the oldest daughter of high wire walker karl wallenda and grandmother of daredevil performer nik wallenda.
