thousands of holidaymakers face up to three days of chaos and cancelled flights because of a strike by france air traffic controllers.
easyjet and ba are among a host of major airlines expected to scrap hundreds of flights thanks to the walk - out , which will start at 5am tomorrow and run for 48 hours - with considerable knock - on disruption expected.
