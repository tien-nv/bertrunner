kenyan has bombed two al-shabaab camps in somalia in the first major military response to last week 's attack by the militant group on a kenyan university that left 148 people dead.
air force jets blitzed compounds in gondodowe and ismail , both in the gedo region bordering kenyan on sunday , a kenya defence forces source said today.
' we targeted the two areas because according to information we have , those ( al-shabaab ) fellows are coming from there to attack kenyan , ' the source said.
kenyan army spokesman , colonel david obonyo , later said : ' the two targets were hit and taken out , the two camps are destroyed
