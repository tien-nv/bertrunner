oil from a russian trawler which was towed out to sea to sink after catching fire in port is washing up on the beaches of gran canaria and threatening tourist spots across the canary islands.
the russian vessel oleg naydenov was carrying 1,400 metric tons of viscous fuel oil , when it caught fire in las palmas port on april 11.
