cancer - stricken basketball player lauren is spending her final days helping to raise money for cancer research and has already accumulated over $ 1.5million.
lauren , 19 , uses a wheelchair and sleeps for many hours at children 's hospital in cincinnati where she is being treated for a brain tumor.
