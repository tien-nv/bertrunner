a brutal raid on the garissa university college in kenyan has left nearly 150 people dead -- including students -- and dozens more wounded.
the harrowing attack comes just months after al-shabaab militants murdered non-muslim workers in a kenyan quarry.
al-shabaab is a somalia group that the americans designated as a foreign terrorist organization in march 2008.
before admitting to the kenyan quarry attack , al-shabaab claimed responsibility for the july 2010 suicide bombings in kampala , uganda , that killed more than 70 people , including a americans citizen , who had gathered at different locations to watch the broadcast of the world cup final soccer match
