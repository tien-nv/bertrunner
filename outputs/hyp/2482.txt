two horses have been rescued after one was found being kept in an alleyway so narrow it could not turn around , while the other was chained to the ground.
rspca inspectors were called to the house after they received numerous calls from residents living nearby in lawrence weston , bristol , who were worried about the animals.
photos taken when they visited show a small horse standing on muddy ground in a passage just over a metre wide , which is blocked at one end with a gate.
