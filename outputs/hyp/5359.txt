while the debate about whether workers prefer a cubicle over an open office layout , a office furniture company has come up with a design that is possibly a happy medium.
steelcase 's the brody worklounge is an effort to preserve the privacy and less distractions given in a cubicle while still being a part of an open office work community.
on its company website , steelcase said the brody worklounge is designed to be ' good for your body and good for your brain '.
the cognitive neuroscience karolinska institute in sweden 's work on flow - a passing state of deep concentration where workers are their most productive - informed the design of brody , according to citylab
