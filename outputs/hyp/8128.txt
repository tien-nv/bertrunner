two women who were part of a group that repeatedly searched mississippi 's capital city for black people to assault were sentenced thursday to multiple years in federal prison for their role in the 2011 hate killing of 47 - year - old james craig anderson.
u.s. district judge wingate on thursday sentenced richards , 21 , of pearl to eight years in prison on one count each of conspiracy to commit a hate crime and concealing the crime by lying to police.
in a separate hearing earlier thursday , wingate also sentenced graves , 22 , of crystal springs to five years in prison for one count of conspiracy to commit a hate crime.
