notts county have appointed moniz as their new manager just a week after the club announced that he was unable to commit to the role.
the former tottenham and hamburg coach pulled out of the running due to ' family reasons ' at the end of march but on tuesday moved to meadow lane on a three - year deal.
the 50 - year - old dutchman has six games to save his new side from relegation into league two , with the magpies currently one point from safety.
