former toffees chairman sir carter has died aged 87.
carter also served as president of the football league and a vice-president of the football association but it is as chairman of toffees throughout the 1980s that he will be remembered.
under his stewardship , the club won two league titles , an fa cup and the european cup winners ' cup , which is still the toffees ' only piece of continental silverware.
