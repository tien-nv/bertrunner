thirty years after the bradford fire which killed 56 football supporters , a new book written by a survivor of the disaster claims it was not an accident.
author fletcher points the finger at the late stafford heginbotham , bradford 's chairman at the time and a businessman who had been linked to eight previous blazes.
' could any man really be as unlucky as stafford heginbotham ? ' writes fletcher , after 15 years spent researching the fire in which four members of his family died.
