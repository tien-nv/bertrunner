there are growing concerns that google 's waze application poses a danger to police because of its ability to track their locations.
the tech giant is now under pressure by top cops to turn off the traffic app 's police - tracking function.
the waze app , which operates like a free gps navigation tool , allows users to tag the locations of parked police vehicles , accidents , congestion , traffic cameras , potholes and more , so that other drivers using waze are warned as they approach the same location.
