the stars were out in full force on sunday evening ahead of the 42nd pfa awards at the grosvenor house in london.
dressed to the nines alongside girlfriend kate gooders , tottenham hotspur striker harry kane appeared excited ahead of the ceremony in which he is nominated for two separate awards , the pfa player of the year and young player of the year.
