a photographer and mother - of - two has turned a collection of candid images of her energetic daughters and their friends into a powerful photography series that celebrates their strength and confidence , while highlighting what it means to be a girl today.
kate t. parker , from atlanta , georgia , was inspired to create her aptly - titled series strong is the new pretty while pouring over photos she snapped of her daughters ella , nine , and alice , six , playing sports and enjoying their childhoods.
' the project became about capturing my girls and their friends as they truly are and how that is ok - not only ok - but worthy of celebration , ' she told daily mail online in an email.
