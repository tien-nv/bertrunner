christian migrants from africa have spoken of making the perilous journey across the med to escape beheading from islamic state fanatics in libya.
a group of eritrean refugees told how they were forced to deny their faith or face death at the hands of islamist gunmen who patrol the towns and beaches of the african state searching for ' infidels '.
haben , 19 , told mailonline : ' we are christian but we had to deny our faith otherwise the gunmen would kill us , slit our throats and cut off our heads.
