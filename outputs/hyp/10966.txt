wikileaks founder assange has agreed to be interviewed by sweden prosecutors in london , his lawyer in sweden told cnn.
assange has been holed up in the ecuadorian embassy in london since june 2012 to avoid extradition to sweden , where prosecutors want to question him about 2010 allegations that he raped one woman and sexually molested another.
according to the lawyer , thomas olsson , sweden prosecutors will now have to reach out to britain and ecuador authorities to request permission to conduct the interview at the embassy
