controversial australian celebrity chef pete evans is under attack again with experts warning his ' reworked ' baby milk formula is dangerous to babies.
dietitians have singled out a ' happy tummy brew ' in pete evans ' cookbook , bubba yum yum : the paleo , for new mums , babies and toddlers , because it contains almost five times the maximum vitamin a dose for a baby.
the original recipe for the ' brew ' contained more than 10 times the maximum daily intake of vitamin a for babies.
' this new infant ' brew ' could seriously harm babies , ' a spokeswoman for the dieticians association of australian told news corp australia
