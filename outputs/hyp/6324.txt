an elderly couple died of carbon monoxide poisoning after lighting a barbecue in their home to keep warm during a power cut , an inquest heard.
grandparents patrick and marianne charles , 78 and 74 , were without heating during a blackout on a cold november night last year.
the couple , who had been married for 53 years , sat down in their conservatory in eastbourne , east sussex , with a glass of wine each and fired up the barbecue to heat up the room.
