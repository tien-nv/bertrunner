thousands remain missing in nepal after a devastating earthquake struck the region on saturday.
a majority of them are nepal , indian and china residents , but a handful are adventurers , trekkers and vacationers who have not been heard from since the catastrophe.
technology has played a huge role in helping families share their worries , ask for help and search for their missing loved ones.
several organizations , such as google and the red cross , have published features about the missing on their websites
